<p><u>Key information for the first day of school</u>
</p>
<ol start="4" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The first two days of the academic year (Thursday, 2 January – Friday,
3 January 2025) will be an orientation programme led by your son’s seniors
and teachers. &nbsp;Key information for the orientation programme are as
follows:
<br>
<br>a. <u>Thursday, 2 January 2025</u>. Please report to the school foyer by <strong>08:20h</strong> in
half uniform (VS PE t-shirt and school khaki shorts). Seniors will direct
your son to his reporting venue. The day’s activities will end by <strong>15:30h</strong>.
Please ensure that your son brings along his school badge and school tie
in preparation for the badge pinning ceremony on Day 2.
<br>
<br>b. <u>Friday, 3 January 2025</u>. Please report to the school by <strong>07:20h </strong>in
his full uniform (white shirt and khaki shorts). The day’s activities will
end by <strong>15:30h</strong>.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Additional information on other activities taking place in January is
shared in the annex for your early information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Further information on the school will be shared with you and all current
parents at a subsequent welcome letter. We wish you a very happy festive
season, and wishing all a very happy new year!</p>
</li>
</ol>
